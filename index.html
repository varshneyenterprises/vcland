ABOUT US

Vidhyaarjan Classes is the brainchild of Er. Dushyant Varshney.

The name VIDHYAARJAN is derived from the Sanskrit words Vidhyaa (Knowledge) and Arjan (To gain); hence the name implies our mission: To Gain Knowledge.

Conceived in 2015 and fully operational since 2016, we have dedicated ourselves to delivering true knowledge to the budding future of tomorrow's India.
